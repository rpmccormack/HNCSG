Graded unit  

exam essentials

  • The exam is going to be held in room 227 under exam conditions and lasts 3 hours.
  • The exam is out of 100 marks and is  in three sections:

Section 1

  • Section 1 is made up of multi-choice questions
  • 15 Questions
  • each qeustion is 1 mark
  • Some of the questions have more than one possible answer but when this is the case it will be clearly shown

Section 2

  • Section 2 has case study style questions
  • Potentially has elements from all four units
  • The section is worth 15 marks

Section 3

  • Section 3 has eight written questions (2 from each unit)
  • You only have to answer 7 of 8 questions
  • Each question is worth 10 marks. 

You need 50% to pass the exam
